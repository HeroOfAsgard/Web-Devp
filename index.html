Questions and Answers

Frequently asked questions

What is DevNest exactly?
DevNest is a high-performance learning ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between "knowing code" and "building industry-grade systems."
What courses do we offer?
We offer a variety of courses including programming, web development, data science, and soft skills training. Our curriculum is designed to meet industry standards and real-world requirements.
Who can enroll in your courses?
Anyone with a willingness to learn can enroll. Whether you're a beginner, student, or working professional, we have courses tailored to different skill levels.
Are the classes online or offline?
We provide both online and offline classes. You can choose the mode that suits your schedule and learning preference.
Do you provide certificates after course completion?
Yes, we provide a certificate of completion for all our courses, which can help enhance your resume and career opportunities.
Do you offer placement assistance?
Yes, we offer placement support including resume building, interview preparation, and job referrals.
What are the class timings?
We offer flexible timings including weekday and weekend batches to accommodate different schedules.
How can I enroll in a course?
You can enroll through our website by filling out the registration form or by visiting our academy in person.
Do you provide demo classes?
Yes, we offer free demo classes so you can experience our teaching style before enrolling.
What is your fee structure?
Our fees are affordable and vary depending on the course. We also offer installment options for selected programs.
